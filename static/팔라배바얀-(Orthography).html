수랕 이나와 (Surat Inawa) bridges our rich native languages with the modern digital world. It is designed to work instantly on any smartphone, laptop, or tablet using standard, built-in Hangeul keyboards, you will never have to install custom apps or download extra fonts just to write. At the same time, the 수랕 이나와 (Surat Inawa) script taps into the brilliant phonetic framework created by King Sejong the Great. By matching his universal design to the natural rhythm of our own Philippine languages, we have created an accessible, future-proof way to express ourselves and record our thoughts.

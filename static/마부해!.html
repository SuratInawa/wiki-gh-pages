수랕 이나와 (Surat Inawa)
(Whispers of the Wind; The Living Script; Inawa script)

수랕 이나와 (Surat Inawa) adapts King Sejong the Great’s phonetic alphabet for the Filipino language, faithfully living by King Sejong's core philosophy to 「write it as it sounds」. This adaptation works seamlessly because King Sejong's original design perfectly accommodates all modern Austronesian Philippine languages and dialects—which total over 200 combined.

The name 수랕 이나와 (Surat Inawa) itself embodies the scientific design and soul of King Sejong the Great’s writing system: the very sounds we make. Translating to Whispers of the Wind or The Living Script, 수랕 이나와 (Surat Inawa) is a writing system intended to carry the words of the Filipino nation to all corners of the world and across time. By preserving the exact phonetic sound of how words are spoken, it allows readers to truly relive the beauty of Philippine languages.

# <span lang="fil-Hang">수랕 이나와</span> (<span lang="fil">Surat Inawa</span>)

## About

**<span lang="fil-Hang">수랕 이나와</span> (<span lang="fil">Surat Inawa</span>)** adapts King <span lang="ko-Latn">Sejong</span> the Great's phonetic alphabet for the Filipino language, faithfully living by King <span lang="ko-Latn">Sejong</span>'s core philosophy to 「write it as it sounds」. This adaptation works seamlessly because King <span lang="ko-Latn">Sejong</span>'s original design perfectly accommodates all modern Austronesian Philippine languages and dialects—which total over 200 combined.

### 🆔 The name

The name <span lang="fil-Hang">수랕 이나와</span> (<span lang="fil">Surat Inawa</span>) itself embodies the scientific design and soul of King <span lang="ko-Latn">Sejong</span> the Great's writing system: the very sounds we make. Translating to **Whispers of the Wind** or **The Living Script**, <span lang="fil-Hang">수랕 이나와</span> (<span lang="fil">Surat Inawa</span>) is a writing system intended to carry the words of the Filipino nation to all corners of the world and across time. By preserving the exact phonetic sound of how words are spoken, it allows readers to truly relive the beauty of Philippine languages.

## 📋 Features

1. Based on the pure <span lang="ko-Latn">Hunminjeongeum</span> system
1. Strictly phonetic
1. Near 1:1 phoneme mapping
1. Matched to the <span lang="fil">Filipino</span> syllable system
1. Built for Austronesian Philippines
1. Digitally ready
1. Simple and intuitive to learn
1. Future-proof expansion

- **Syllabic blocks:** It organizes characters into distinct syllabic blocks rather than letters.
- **Universal:** It can accurately transcribe borrowed words from any language, such as "news" and "fighting".
- **Phonetic:** It captures and preserves the actual native sound and pronunciation.
- **Legibility:** There is no need to magnify the text to read small, easily missed vowel markers (diacritics).
- **Generally shorter:** It yields structurally shorter written words and sentences
